PHONE REMOTE SAFETY
===================

Use phone LAN mode only on your private Wi-Fi.
Do not port-forward this dashboard.
Do not expose it to the public internet.

Allowed phone actions in this build:
- View dashboard.
- View Market Feed.
- View Growth/Learning Pulse.
- Manual Pulse Now.
- Start/Pause Overnight Watch.
- Generate Morning Report.
- Scan Update Dock.
- Apply valid safe update ZIPs from UPDATE_INBOX.
- Rollback overwritten files from last safe update.

Not available in this build:
- Live trading.
- Champion approval.
- API key changes.
- Risk increase.
- Risk Police disable.
- Proof-gate bypass.
