Backtest Walk-Forward Policy V015A

Backtests use only candles collected by Bali's public-data candle harvester.
A run must be treated as research evidence only.
No strategy may claim champion status from one run.
No live trading, API keys, private endpoints, or broker/exchange execution are added.
Fees and slippage are included in replay calculations.
The champion gate remains locked until repeated evidence and human approval exist.
